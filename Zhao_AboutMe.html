My name is Lilian Zhao and I am currently a Dublin High School student. I aspire to work in the field of geology, and I plan on reaching this goal by taking science courses in high school and majoring in geology in college. This is my career choice because it is something I’m interested in and because of that, I would be able to do it well.

In 8th grade, we had to work on a "Civics Actions Project". It was a project that took about 5 months and we had to identify a problem, research, and find a way to solve it. We had to think analytically, stay organized, and work well in a team. These are skills that would be useful in working in geology. At first I didn’t want to do the project, but I actually ended up enjoying it and it helped me learn how to better the community and to take action to solve problems.

This year, I am in the Technovation Club at DHS, where you create an app to help solve a problem. I am looking forward to it because it’s similar to the CAP project.

Thank you for your time.
